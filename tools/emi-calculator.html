EMI Kya Hota Hai? 🤔

EMI = Equated Monthly Installment. Har mahine aap bank ko jo paisa dete ho wo EMI kehlata hai. EMI mein principal aur interest dono hote hain.

EMI Formula

EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1)
  • P = Loan amount
  • r = Monthly interest rate
  • n = Total months
